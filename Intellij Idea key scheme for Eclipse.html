I joined http://sonoasystems.com/ and working on eclipse plugin development. I had to switch to eclipse from Intellij Idea, to take advantage of eclipse PDE support. I didn't find any key scheme of intellij idea distributed with eclipse. So I created one myself. If anybody interested, you can download it here:

sources and plugin

Drop the plugin jar in eclipse/plugins folder and restart eclipse. Now in preferences dialog under General > Keys you can find "Intellij Idea" key scheme.
Hope this will be useful to idea fans who has to get their hands dirty with eclipse ;)
